About Me

I am Joshua David Pachejo, known as Josh, an aspiring film director and photographer. Since my Grandmother gifted me a DSLR camera, I have been captivated by the technical and creative aspects of film and photography.

My journey started as a school event photographer, where I gained experience capturing moments and telling stories through images. As I continue to grow in this field, I am committed to honing my skills and sharing my vision with the world.

Home Town

Antipolo City

Antipolo is a city east of Manila in the Philippines. It's known as a Catholic pilgrimage site. In the Antipolo Cathedral, the shrine to Our Lady of Peace and Good Voyage has a 17th-century statue of the Virgin Mary, and attracts thousands of visitors each year. Southwest, the Pinto Art Museum displays contemporary Filipino paintings and sculpture in tropical gardens and whitewashed, Mediterranean-style villas.
